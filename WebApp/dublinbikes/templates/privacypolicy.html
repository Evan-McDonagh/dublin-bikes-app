King Kogs Dublin Privacy Policy

This privacy policy will explain how our organization uses the personal data we collect from you when you use our website.

What data do we collect?

King Kogs Dublin collects the following data:

  • Email address
  • Your favourited Dublin Bikes Stations

How do we collect your data?

You directly provide King Kogs Dublin with all of the data we collect. We collect data and process data when you:

  • Register online
  • Add stations to your favourites
  • Login, via your browser’s cookies.

How will we use your data?

King Kogs Dublin collects your data so that we can:

  • Manage your account.
  • Email you to allow a  password reset

How do we store your data?

King Kogs Dublin securely stores your data on a secure server in Ireland.

King Kogs Dublin will keep your email address until you delete your account. Once your account is deleted, your data will persist in a backup medium for up to 60 days, after which it will be permanently deleted.

What are your data protection rights?

King Kogs Dublin would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

The right to access – You have the right to request King Kogs Dublin for copies of your personal data.

The right to rectification – You have the right to request that King Kogs Dublin correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request King Kogs Dublin to complete the information you believe is incomplete.

The right to erasure – You have the right to request that King Kogs Dublin erase your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to restrict processing – You have the right to request that King Kogs Dublin restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to object to processing – You have the right to object to King Kogs Dublin’s processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to data portability – You have the right to request that King Kogs Dublin transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at our email:

Write to us: conorlshort@gmail.com

Cookies

Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard Internet log information and visitor behaviour information. When you visit our website, we may collect information from you automatically through cookies or similar technology

For further information, visit allaboutcookies.org.

How do we use cookies?

King Kogs Dublin uses cookies only in the following way:

  • Keeping you signed in

What types of cookies do we use?

There are a number of different types of cookies, however, our website uses:

  • Functionality – King Kogs Dublin uses these cookies so that we recognize you on our website and remember your previously selected preferences.

How to manage cookies

You can set your browser not to accept cookies, and the above website tells you how to remove cookies from your browser. However, in a few cases, some of our website features may not function as a result.

Changes to our privacy policy

King Kogs Dublin keeps its privacy policy under regular review and places any updates on this web page. This privacy policy was last updated on 15 April 2020.

How to contact us

If you have any questions about King Kogs Dublin’s privacy policy, the data we hold on you, or you would like to exercise one of your data protection rights, please do not hesitate to contact us.
